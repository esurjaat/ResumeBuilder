## Steps

1.  **Resume \> Input.** Download template *OR* fill out survey.

2.  **Resume \> Build.** Upload filled out template or choose *Survey*
    as the source.

3.  **Resume \> Build.** Push the *Generate* button to download the
    Resume in pdf format.
